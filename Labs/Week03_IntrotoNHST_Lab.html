Power Analysis

An a priori power analysis should be conducted when designing the study in order to determine the number of participants needed to detect the effect of interest. In this course, we’ll be using WebPower to conduct our power analyses. We’ll begin to conduct power analyses when we run specific statistical tests. For today, we’ll focus on improving your understanding of the concept of statistical power. Have a look at the following power curves and spend some time trying to interpret them.
